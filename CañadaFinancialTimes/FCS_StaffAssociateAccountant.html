Articles: Finance Career Spotlight: Staff/Associate Accountant

Meighen, Lukas

Description

Staff accountants perform various tasks such as maintaining general ledger, preparing financial statements, and auditing things such as balance sheets, income statements, and cash flows for large publicly traded companies as well as private and non-profit companies.

Average National Salary

$70,000

Typical Prerequisites​

  • Bachelor's Degree
  • Post-Graduate degree (Master's)
  • Some form of finance related job experience (internship)

Insight From An Experienced Staff Member


What does a Typical Day Look Like?

I would go to different client offices and audit the financial statements as well as systems of controls over loss etc. I would also prepare certain areas on the financial statements (e.g. fixed assets and liabilities) for review by senior associates.

What Qualities Make You Successful In This Field?

Curiousness is needed, you need to be able to ask why a certain decision was made or why something is happening. Skepticism is needed, you cannot accept what the company accepts without proof. It is also important to be personable, you are not going to get help from your client unless they can work with you. The last thing is Knowing your subject matter (accounting principles). It is crucial that you are able to apply the right principles/rules in a given situation. ​

What Did Your Path To This Career Look Like?

Community College (CSM) while working part time —to UC Berkeley as an undergrad in ECON —to Law School (Santa Clara)—to working for lawyers —to deciding law was not what I wanted to do —back to school for accounting (San Francisco State) to a job with a Big 4 public accounting firm (KPMG)
