Articles: How To Prioritize Your Budget

Contreras, Sandy

Introduction​

Can you tell the difference between the things you want and need? Some may confidently think to themselves, “Yes”, but others know the truth. Here are some tips to help you prioritize where to put your money and when it absolutely needs to be spent/saved.​

What to prioritize with your budget​

  • Deciding how to allocate your money
  • Defining financial goals
  • Not letting yourself go over the budget ​
  • Learn how to save financial aid for important things instead of going shopping​ ​
  • Keep track of expenses​ ​

How Can You Use This?

A lot of us go overboard when it comes to spending money since some of us were never thought about how to spend and save our money. It is very important how to allocate your money such as investing for your future, for example starting life insurance for retirement. Defining financial goals such as what are things you would like to pay off to start saving money. A lot of us go overboard when it comes to shopping it is very important to remind yourself what are the things you need instead of wasting money on things you want. ​

Who does not love when financial aid comes in, it is very important how we manage the money-saving up for some of the financial goals we have in mind, instead of shopping for unnecessary objects. Finally, it is very important the way you keep track of your expenses, this may include things like how much money you spend in a month and what is your goal.
